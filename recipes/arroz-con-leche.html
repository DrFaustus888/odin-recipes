Arroz con Leche

Double the recipe, trust me.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup short grain rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • a pinch of salt
  • powdered cinnamon to taste
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Whisk together the cans of condensed and evaporated milk and add the vanilla. Put in fridge.
  2. In a saucepan, mix the water, rice, a couple shakes of cinnamon, and the salt. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat, cover and simmer until the rice is tender and the water is mostly absorbed.
  4. When the rice is done, stir in the milk mixture.
  5. Cook uncovered over medium low heat. Keep stirring so that it doesn't stick.
  6. Once the pudding is thick and you can see the grains of rice, remove from heat.
  7. Put the pudding into a bowl, sprinkle it with cinnamon and chill in the fridge for at least one hour.
