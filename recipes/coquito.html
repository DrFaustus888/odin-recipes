Coquito

Make sure you're in for the night!

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 cups white or spiced rum (or to taste)
  • 2 cans coconut cream
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Powdered cinnamon to taste

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients in a blender
  2. Refrigerate and serve cold. Garnish with powdered cinnamon.
