Tostones

Super yum stuff.

Ingredients

  • 2 large green plantains
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the plantains and cut them into diagonal slices about 3/4 inch thick.
  2. Heat 1 inch of oil over medium heat in a heavy pot.
  3. Fry the plantains, flipping them until they are tender but not crisp, about 4 minutes.
  4. Transfer them to paper towel to drain and cool.
  5. Smush them flat with a wooden spoon.
  6. Fry them again, flipping them for about 2 minutes, until they are golden brown.
  7. Transfer them to paper towel again to drain and cool.
  8. Sprinkle with salt to taste.
